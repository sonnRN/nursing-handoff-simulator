# Feedback Guide

Thank you for reviewing this demo.

## Good Feedback Topics

- Does the handoff summary feel clinically relevant?
- Is the prioritization explainable?
- Are the safety boundaries clear?
- Is the architecture understandable?
- Are the synthetic data labels obvious enough?
- What would make this more credible as a research prototype?

## Please Include

- what page or flow you reviewed
- what you expected
- what actually happened
- whether the issue is about logic, wording, UI clarity, or safety framing
- screenshots only if they contain synthetic data only

## Please Do Not Include

- real patient data
- copied text from live EMRs
- credentials or internal endpoints
- screenshots from production clinical systems

## Suggested Issue Labels

- `clinical-feedback`
- `safety`
- `documentation`
- `ui`
- `data`
- `release`

## Reviewer Audiences

This repo is especially interested in feedback from:

- nurses and clinical educators
- health informatics reviewers
- developers and open-source contributors
- healthcare AI safety reviewers
